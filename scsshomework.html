Venice Islands
Giudecca is one of the Venice islands that visitors to the city will do well to keep in mind. This tranquil Venetian Lagoon island and working-class neighborhood got its start as a fishing village in and around 500 AD. It later transformed into a rather upscale enclave in the 1800s, thanks to the addition of luxurious homes. and even a movie studio was eventually established here.
